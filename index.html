Protective Styles

Protective styles are ones that don't consist of the natural hair being out loose, which is where the 'protective' part comes in.

Types of Protective Styles

There are several different types of protective styles. These styles are important because it protects the ends of your hair which is the oldest and most fragile part of the strand.

Different Types of Protective Styles

  • Braids
  • Twists
  • Wigs

My Favorite Protective Style

My favorite protective style is twists. Most twists are easy to do, they are stylish, and they come in a variety of types.

Top Three Favorite Twist Styles

  2. Senegalese
  3. Flat
